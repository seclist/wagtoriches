Our Adoption Process

Our simple, straightforward process ensures the perfect match between you and your new furry family member. We're here to guide you every step of the way.

1

Browse & Enquire

View our available dogs and complete an adoption enquiry form if you're interested in a particular dog.

2

Application Review

Our team reviews your application and matches your lifestyle with the dog's needs to ensure the perfect fit.

3

Meet & Greet

If it's a good match, we arrange a meeting so you can get to know the dog and ask any questions.

4

Home Visit

We conduct a home visit to ensure your environment is suitable and safe for the dog.

5

Adoption

Once approved, we finalize the adoption and provide ongoing support as your new family member settles in.

Our Team

Meet the passionate individuals dedicated to finding every dog their perfect forever home.

DT

Dawn Topping

Director & Rehoming Coordinator

With over 15 years of experience in animal welfare, Dawn leads our CIC mission to ensure every dog transition is smooth, safe, and permanent. Her dedication to matching the right dog with the right family has helped hundreds of dogs find their forever homes. Dawn's compassionate approach and attention to detail make her an invaluable part of our team.

SM

Sarah Mitchell

Veterinary Liaison & Care Coordinator

Sarah ensures all our dogs receive the highest standard of veterinary care and support. With a background in veterinary nursing, she coordinates health checks, vaccinations, and ongoing medical care for all dogs in our program. Sarah's expertise and caring nature ensure our dogs are healthy, happy, and ready for their new families.

JM

James Morgan

Adoption Support & Home Visit Coordinator

James coordinates our home visits and provides ongoing support to families throughout the adoption process. His background in social work and genuine love for dogs helps him assess potential homes and provide guidance to new adopters. James ensures that every adoption is successful and that both dogs and families have the support they need.
